Kanga's Climb

Journey to the Sky Garden

Help Kanga climb from the polluted depths to the clean Sky Garden to save her sick joey and heal the environment!

How to Play

HOLD SPACE - Charge your jump

RELEASE SPACE - Jump!

A/D or Arrow Keys - Aim left/right while charging

Goal: Climb from the polluted bottom to the clean Sky Garden at the top!

Warning: Falling sends you way back down - be careful!
